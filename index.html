Marketing Strategy

Strategic Direction â 2026

This retainer is built around consistent, quality output rather than volume. The following priorities guide work across the full engagement period.

01

Keep the Brand Visible and Trusted

Monthly newsletters keep the brand in front of existing customers and warm leads throughout the year. Consistent, professional communication reinforces authority in the AED space and builds long-term trust with subscribers who may not be ready to purchase immediately but will be.

02

Capitalise on Key Purchase Windows

EOFY (May/June) is the primary campaign focus for 2026. Secondary opportunities include the new financial year (July), the spring sport season (September/October), and the end-of-year budget cycle (November). Bimonthly ad campaigns are timed to align with these moments.

03

Lead with Saves and Real Stories

Cardiac arrest saves are among the most powerful content available. Newsletters should feature real saves, community stories, and testimonials wherever possible. This builds emotional connection and demonstrates real-world impact far more effectively than product specifications alone.

04

Target Decision-Makers, Not Just Browsers

Facebook campaigns should target workplace managers, HR professionals, club administrators, and facilities managers â the people with authority to purchase. B2B messaging around compliance, liability, and duty of care resonates with this audience and drives higher quality enquiries.

05

Maintain a Clean, Reliable Website

The website is the primary conversion point. Keeping it fast, functional, and properly integrated is non-negotiable. Broken integrations or outdated content will undermine every other channel. Ongoing maintenance ensures the site supports rather than hinders sales.

06

Build Organic Visibility Through Content

Publishing newsletter articles to the website builds a library of relevant, searchable content over time. Consistent publishing in a niche like AEDs compounds steadily and improves organic search visibility without requiring a separate SEO retainer.

Additional Opportunities: If the business wants to grow beyond this retainer, the following are worth discussing â organic social media posting (2 to 4 posts per week), Google Ads reactivation, a CPR awareness content series, or a referral program targeting allied health and first responder networks.

These are not included here but can be scoped and added at any point during the engagement.
